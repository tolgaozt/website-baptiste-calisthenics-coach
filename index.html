RISE FROM NOTHING

I’m Baptiste Auvray, born on November 22, 2002, in Blois, a small town in the heart of France.
I started calisthenics in 2020, after three years spent building my strength from the ground up.

Over time, I learned, progressed, and unlocked key movements such as the handstand, muscle-up, handstand push-ups, front lever, the 90° planche, and the full planche.

Little by little, I developed my own style by creating unique flows and combinations that reflect my vision and approach to this sport.

Driven by the desire to share my journey, I designed two complete training programs, accessible to different levels. Their goal : to guide you step by step toward mastering two of the most iconic calisthenics movements : the handstand push-up and the 90° planche.
